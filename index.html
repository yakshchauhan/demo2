The Problem

Why most influencer campaigns fail.

The influencer space is full of noise — random partnerships, inflated metrics, and campaigns that look active but don't drive results. Most brands have been here before.

Poor creator selection

Choosing creators by follower count alone — ignoring audience quality, niche fit, and content style.

Misaligned audiences

Content reaches the wrong people. High impressions, low impact. The audience doesn't convert.

Lack of structured execution

No clear brief, no defined deliverables, no timeline. The campaign drifts and underdelivers.

No measurable outcomes

Campaigns close with vanity metrics and no actionable data. Nothing to optimize from.
